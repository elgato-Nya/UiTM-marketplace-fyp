Design Enhancements

✓ M-Shaped Structure

Outer pillars are tallest, middle pillar is shortest → forms the letter "M" for MarKet. Instantly recognizable shape.

✓ Floating Middle Pillar

The center pillar doesn't reach the bottom, creating visual "floating" effect. Represents the marketplace as a connection point between buyers (left) and sellers (right).

✓ Terminal Connection Nodes

Small circles at key points suggest network connectivity and tech infrastructure (matching Nekodez's coding aesthetic).

✓ Wider Pillars

Increased from 20px to 24px for better visibility at small sizes (favicons, mobile).

✓ Softer Corner Radius

Increased from rx="4" to rx="6" for a more approachable, friendly feel (university community).

✓ Strategic Proportions

Outer pillars: 88px tall. Middle pillar: 44px (exactly half). Creates perfect visual balance and "M" shape.
